A

Alloy
A mix of molten metals combined in a crucible to an exact ratio. Ratios →
Anthracite
The hottest natural coal and the longest-burning fuel. Fuels →
Anvil
Where hot metal is smithed. Works its own tier and one above — a bronze anvil shapes iron. Anvil →
Anvil tier
The metal an anvil is made of, gating what it can smith: copper→bronze, bronze→iron, iron→steel. Tiers →

B

Bellows
Pumped air that lets a forge reach full heat — without one, fuel hits only ~60%. Forge →
Blister steel
The raw output of the cementation furnace; must be hammered into steel. Steel →
Bloom (iron bloom)
The spongy lump a bloomery produces; hammered on an anvil into wrought-iron ingots. Bloomery →
Bloomery
The built furnace that smelts iron ore into a bloom. Bloomery →
Bog iron
Surface limonite in wetlands — the easiest first iron. Ores →
Bowtorn
A ranged rift creature with a turret attack. Bestiary →
Brass
Copper + zinc; a decorative alloy (lanterns, fittings), not a tool metal. Alloys →
Brigandine
Leather-and-plate metal armor, available from the copper age. Armor →
Bronze
Copper alloyed with tin, or zinc+bismuth, or gold+silver. The gate to iron. Alloying →

C

Cassiterite
Tin ore — scarce; the bronze-age bottleneck. Ores →
Cellar
A cool, sealed room that slows food spoilage; scored 100% − skylight − cooling. Cellar →
Cementation furnace
Carburizes iron into blister steel over ~6.67 in-game days. Steel →
Charcoal pit
Firewood buried in soil and smouldered into charcoal; seal within ~30 s. Charcoal →
Chisel
Tool for fine voxel sculpting of placed blocks — detailing builds and decor. Crafting →
Claymold
A clay-formed mold poured with molten metal to cast a tool or ingot. Casting →
Clay forming
The voxel minigame that shapes pottery from clay before firing. Clay forming →
Cob
A cheap build block of clay mixed with grass; quick early walls. Homestead →
Coke
Coal baked airtight in a coke oven; the hottest practical fuel. Fuels →
Commoner
The default class — no bonuses, no penalties. Classes →
Compost
Rotted matter cured in a barrel (~20 days); fertiliser that restores all three nutrients. Soil →
Cooking pot
A fired clay pot for multi-ingredient meals on a fire pit. Cooking →
Crock
A pottery container; sealed, it slows spoilage dramatically. Preservation →
Crucible
A fire-clay vessel that melts ore for casting. Crucible →

D

Damage tier
Attacks rate 1–3; armor reduces by flat amount then percent. Combat →
Density search
Propick mode reporting an area's ore probability in ‰. Prospecting →
Devastation
A blighted, low-stability biome where the Rust world bleeds through; dense rifts & hostiles. Temporal →
Drifter
The core temporal hostile; six tiers; spawns at light ≤7 and in storms. Bestiary →
Drifter tiers
Surface · Deep · Tainted · Corrupt · Nightmare · Double-headed (storm-only) — each tougher than the last. Stats →

E – F

Electrum
A decorative gold+silver alloy. Alloys →
Fennel · Licorice
New 1.22 aromatic crops; ingredients in fishing stinkbait. Crops →
Fire pit
Your first cooker and smelter; bellowed, it melts copper. Fire →
Firestarter
A bow-drill made from sticks + dry grass to light fires. Fire →
Fishing
New 1.22 activity — rods, lures & traps for 20+ freshwater fish (protein). Fishing →
Flax → linen
Crop processed into twine and linen for clothing. Materials →
Forge
Heats metal to working temperature for the anvil (1.22: bellows, tongs, temper). Forge →

G – H

Galena
Ore yielding lead and silver. Ores →
Gambeson
Light quilted-cloth armor; a strong, cheap base layer (tier 2, 70%). Armor →
Greenhouse
A glazed structure that adds +5 °C, letting crops grow through winter. World →
Grinding wheel
New 1.22 station that sharpens & repairs tool heads to improve them. Metalworking →
Helve
The wooden haft/handle of the helve hammer; the powered head it swings. Mechanical power →
Helve hammer
Mechanically powered hammer that automates working blooms and blister steel. Mechanical power →
Hyena
Tier-2 pack predator of warm regions; ~10-block aggro. Predators →

I – K

Igneous
One of three rock families (granite, basalt, andesite…); includes deepest kimberlite. Rocks →
Ingot
100 units of metal = 20 ore nuggets; supplies 42 smithing voxels. Units →
Iron
Smelted in a bloomery, hammered from a bloom; needs a bronze pick to mine & bronze anvil to work. Iron age →
Kimberlite
The deepest rock layer — the only host of diamonds. Rocks →
Knapping
Chipping loose stone into tool heads — the first craft. Knapping →

L – M

Limonite
Iron ore; the bog/surface form is the easiest early iron. Ores →
Locust
Metal cave insectoid; the Clockmaker class counters it. Bestiary →
Meteoric iron
Premium iron-class metal from meteorites; needs an iron pick. Metals →
Molds
Fired clay forms you pour molten copper/bronze into to cast tools. Casting →

N – Q

Node search
Propick mode giving the direction & size of a nearby ore node. Prospecting →
Nutrition
Five food groups (fruit/veg/protein/grain/dairy) that raise max health. Nutrition →
Pit kiln
A burn that fires raw pottery hard enough to use (~10–20 h). Clay forming →
Prospecting pick
The tool that reads ore density (region) and nodes (deposit). Prospecting →
Pulverizer
Mechanically powered crusher; boosts ore yield and feeds steel. Mechanical power →
Quern
Hand- or power-driven mill that grinds grain into flour. Mechanical power →

R – S

Rift
A temporal tear that spawns hostiles and drains stability ~3%/s nearby. Rifts →
Rust world
The decaying reality bleeding through at zero stability. Temporal →
Saturation
The energy bar (cap 1500) that drains as you act. Nutrition →
Smithing
Hammering hot metal on an anvil to a Handbook blueprint. Smithing →
Soil fertility
Per-plot N/P/K that crops drain; managed by rotation & fertilizer. Farming →
Steel
Carburized iron — the top metal tier. Steel →
Storage vessel
Bulk pottery container; slows spoilage (×0.75 veg, ×0.5 grain). Storage →

T – W

Temporal gear
Drifter/trader drop; restores +30% stability and powers translocators. Gears →
Temporal stability
How firmly reality holds (0–150%; 100% neutral). Temporal →
Temporal storm
A telegraphed event spawning drifters everywhere; every ~10–20 days. Storms →
Terra preta
The richest soil (80% fertility); crafted. Crops →
Translocator
A repaired teleporter linking two distant points. Locations →
Tin bronze
88–92% Cu + 8–12% Sn — the strongest common bronze. Alloys →
Waterwheel
New 1.22 mechanical generator driven by flowing water. Mechanical power →
Windmill
Sail-driven mechanical generator. Mechanical power →
Wrought iron
The worked-iron ingot you get by hammering a bloom. Bloomery →
