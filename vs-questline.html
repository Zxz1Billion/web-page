The Questline

Vintage Story doesn't ship a quest book — so here's one. Ten chapters of tickable objectives carry you from a bewildered seraph with empty hands to a steel-clad survivor who weathers temporal storms and walks the translocator network. Each quest names a clear goal, its concrete tasks, and the reward it unlocks. Tick as you play; it all saves here.

10 chapters ~30 quests Browser-saved Stone → Steel → Story

★ — Read me first

How This Works

This is a companion quest book, not an in-game mod. It mirrors how FTB Quests structures a modpack: chapters unlock roughly in order, each quest has tasks to complete and a reward (here, the capability you've just earned).

  • Tick the tasks as you do them in-game. The meter below counts every task in the whole book and saves to this browser — close the tab and come back; your ticks persist.
  • Chapters gate each other the way Vintage Story's tech does: you can't really start Bronze before Copper, or Steel before Iron. Each chapter notes its prerequisite.
  • Every quest links the deep-dive page that explains the how. This book is the checklist; the rest of the Almanac is the manual.

Chapter 1

Stone age · no prerequisite

Awakening

You arrive with nothing. Make the first tools, the first fire, and live to see Day Two. Full walkthrough: The First Day.

Quest · Tools

🔪 First Edges

Knap your way out of the Stone Age's first hour.

Reward The ability to harvest grass, reeds, food & wood — the start of everything.

Quest · Survival

🔥 Light Against the Dark

Make fire and a place to hide before nightfall.

Reward A safe base camp and your first full night survived.

Quest · Food

🍖 First Forage

Don't starve, don't poison yourself.

Reward A stocked belly and the first taste of the nutrition system (The Larder).

Chapter 2

Stone age · requires Ch.1

Earth & Fire

No metal happens without pottery. Dig clay, shape it, and fire it into the crucible and molds that the whole tech tree depends on. How-to: The Four Crafts → Clay Forming.

Quest · Pottery

🏺 The Potter

Turn raw clay into useful, fired vessels.

Reward Food storage, water-carrying, and a working pit kiln.

Quest · Metalprep

⚗️ The Crucible

Make the tools that let you handle molten metal.

Reward You can now smelt & cast metal — the gateway to the Copper Age.

Quest · Comfort

🍲 The First Pot

Cook real meals, not just roasts.

Reward Better nutrition, a saved spawn point, real comfort.

Chapter 3

Copper age · requires Ch.2

The Red Metal

Copper is the real start of the game: stone can't mine ore, so a cast copper pickaxe unlocks mining itself. Deep-dive: Fire & Metal and casting.

Quest · Prospect

🪙 First Strike

Find your first copper.

Reward Enough copper to pour your first tools.

Quest · Cast

🌋 The First Pour

Smelt and cast copper.

Reward A pickaxe — you can finally mine ore veins.

Quest · Smith

🛠️ The Smith Awakens

Stand up your first metalworking station.

Reward An anvil + forge: the foundation for all smithing to come.

Chapter 4

Bronze age · requires Ch.3

The Alloy Age

Copper alone won't reach iron — you need a bronze anvil first. Gather alloy metals and mix them in the crucible. Recipes: Fire & Metal → Alloying.

Quest · Gather

⛏️ Gather the Alloys

Find what copper needs to become bronze.

Reward The ingredients for whichever bronze your map offers.

Quest · Alloy

🔶 Bronze Casting

Mix and pour bronze.

Reward A bronze anvil — the gate that lets you work iron.

Quest · Defend

🛡️ First Armor

Stop dying to wolves.

Reward Real protection — see Warmth & Wear.

Chapter 5

Iron age · requires Ch.4

The Bloom

Iron isn't cast — it's smelted into a bloom and hammered into shape. First you need charcoal. Deep-dive: Fire & Metal → Bloomery.

Quest · Fuel

🪵 Charcoal Burner

Make the high-heat fuel iron demands.

Reward A charcoal stockpile — fuel for bloomery, forge & steel.

Quest · Smelt

🔩 The Bloomery

Pull iron out of the earth.

Reward Wrought iron — tools & armor a tier above bronze.

Quest · Equip

⚒️ Iron Outfit

Re-tool in iron.

Reward An iron anvil — the prerequisite for the Steel Age.

Chapter 6

Steel age · requires Ch.5

The Crucible of Steel

The endgame metal. Carburize iron in a cementation furnace into blister steel, then hammer it into steel. Deep-dive: Fire & Metal → Steel.

Quest · Industry

🏭 Industrial Heat

Scale up your iron pipeline.

Reward Faster iron and the crushing step steelmaking needs.

Quest · Carburize

🔥 Cementation

Make steel the historical way.

Reward Steel — the top tier of tools and armor.

Quest · Master

🗡️ Steel-Clad

Wear the crown of the tech tree.

Reward The strongest gear in the game. You've "beaten" metallurgy.

Chapter 7

Stop grinding by hand. Build a mechanical network and let wind or water do the work. Deep-dive: The Turning World.

Quest · Generate
