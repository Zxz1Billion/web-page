What's New in 1.22

The 1.22 release (April 2026) is large: 700+ features, tweaks and fixes. It adds a fishing overhaul to the post-1.21 water system, rebuilds the forge and metalworking loop, adds waterwheels to mechanical power, drops dynamic dungeons into worldgen, and modernises storage with voxel-placed cabinets. Each change below links to the chapter that explains it.

01 — New activity

Fishing

Fishing gives lakes and rivers a use. There are 20+ freshwater fish species with location-specific populations and bait/lure preferences.

  • Gear: craft fishing rods (active), lures, and passive traps.
  • Bait: earthworms (collected via the new worm grunting), raw-meat baits, and stinkbait made with the new fennel & licorice aromatics.
  • After the catch: fish are protein; cook or dry them. Mount trophies with the new taxidermy.
Covered in depth

Full fishing how-to and where it fits in your diet is in The Larder → Fishing.

02 — The big rework

Forge & Metalworking

Smithing got a ground-up overhaul. It adds depth at the forge.

What changed at the forge
AdditionWhat it does
BellowsPump air to reach full heat. Without one, fuel hits only ~60% of its max temperature.
Metal tongsHold hot work safely and move it between forge and anvil.
Fuel types matterCharcoal, coke and the coals now differ in heat and burn time.
Quenching & temperingQuench in water, then temper. Trade durability for extra power on tool heads.
Cementation steelSteel is made by carburizing iron in a cementation furnace (blister steel → hammered).
Covered in depth

The whole heat ladder (fuels, forge, bloomery, blast furnace and steel) is in Fire & Metal. The hammering minigame is in The Four Crafts.

03 — New mechanisms

Mechanical Power

  • Waterwheel: a new generator driven by rapidly flowing water, added alongside rapid-flow water blocks and a small 4-way hub.
  • The large windmill and spur gears expand what you can build and how power is split.
  • Helve hammer fix: it no longer has an exponential power cost as speed rises. Networks no longer oscillate, so automation is far more stable.
Covered in depth

Generators, transmission and a power budget are in The Turning World.

04 — Home & hearth

Storage & Decoration

  • Cabinets: modern storage placed with voxel precision, so they fit your build exactly.
  • Mannequins: display your clothing and armor sets.
Covered in depth

Storage options and where they fit a base are in The Homestead → Storage; clothing & armor in Warmth & Wear.

05 — Worldgen

Dynamic Dungeons

A dynamic dungeon now generates in Survival worlds. It is a procedurally assembled, multi-room structure with loot and threats, and the layout varies each time rather than using a fixed prefab.

Covered in depth

Approach it with steel armour, light and food. See Ruins & Wayfarers.

06 — The long tail

Flora & Fauna

  • Eight mushroom varieties added to foraging.
  • Two new crops, fennel & licorice: aromatics that also feed the new fishing stinkbait.
  • Boar variants added to the wild-pig family.
  • Plus hundreds of smaller tweaks and fixes across the 1.22.x point releases.
Covered in depth

Crops are in The Larder; the animals in The Bestiary.

07 — Before you patch

Updating Safely

  1. Back up your save. Major versions change worldgen and mechanics. Copy your world folder before launching the new version.
  2. Update your mods. If you run mods, confirm each has a 1.22-compatible build before loading a modded world.
  3. Expect new structures only in new chunks. Dynamic dungeons and new ores/flora appear in freshly generated terrain. Travel to unexplored land to find them.
  4. Read the official patch notes for the exact list and any save-migration notes. They're the final word over this summary.
