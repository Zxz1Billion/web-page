Keeps & Galleons

When Dungeons Arise studs the world with huge, lavishly hand-built structures — mountain keeps, sky aviaries, jungle treehouses — and its Seven Seas companion floats undead galleons and forts across the oceans. They're gorgeous, loot-rich, and well-guarded.

Lavish structures + Seven Seas MC 1.19.2 · Forge

00 — Orientation

Start Here

These aren't little ruins — they're set-piece buildings the size of a small base, full of rooms, mobs and chests. Treat each one as a mini-dungeon: a place to gear up, not a free loot drop.

It's a centerpiece of the Cartographer's Journal. Bring real gear.

01 — On land

02 — On the water

Seven Seas (Naval)

The Seven Seas add-on takes the action offshore with ocean structures:

  • Pirate galleons & undead ships — multi-deck vessels crewed by hostiles; board carefully and fight deck to deck.
  • Lighthouses & naval forts — coastal/island strongholds with vertical layouts.
  • Small & large pirate ships — a range of sizes and loot.

Sail out by boat to reach them; mind the water between you and a safe retreat.

03 — The hunt

Finding Them

They generate naturally across the world and oceans. The best approach is to explore widely — overland for keeps, by boat for Seven Seas — and use a map mod to mark each one. Spawn frequency is config-tunable, so density varies by pack. See the Operator's Handbook for JourneyMap/Map Atlases.

04 — The reward

Loot & Danger

Inside you'll find chests with mid-tier gear, enchanted books and resources, scaled by how deep and dangerous the structure is. The mob density is real — these are designed as combat encounters, so the loot is earned, not gifted. Exact loot tables are config-driven.

05 — The method

Clearing a Structure

  1. Approach & scout. Note entrances and the vertical layout before charging in.
  2. Light it up and fight room by room; watch for ambushes and drops between levels.
  3. Bring the kit: solid armour/weapon (Tinkers / Apotheosis), healing/food (Farmer's Delight), and a grave net (Tombstone).
  4. Sweep for chests — they're hidden through the whole build, not just one vault.

06 — Bonus

Building Inspiration

Beyond loot, these structures are superb architecture references. Note their palettes, rooflines and room layouts for your own projects — then recreate them faster with the tools in the Pattern Book (Chipped palettes, Building Gadgets, Supplementaries).

07 — Field notes

Tips

  • Explore by boat to find Seven Seas structures; keep a retreat route across the water.
  • Mark cleared structures on your map so you don't re-raid an empty keep.
  • Loot scales with depth/danger — push into the guarded inner rooms for the best chests.
