The Questline

A chapter-by-chapter questline for To the Moon 2.0, in the spirit of an FTB Quests book: eight chapters of concrete objectives and rewards that march you from your first night to a rocket on the pad. Tick the boxes as you complete each task — they're plain checkboxes, so your progress sticks while the page is open.

00 — Orientation

How Quests Work

This is a companion questline you can follow even if the pack's built-in FTB Quests book is laid out differently — think of it as a self-guided checklist that ties the whole pack together.

  • Each chapter has a short list of quests: an objective, a reason, and a suggested reward (the milestone you unlock by doing it).
  • Chapters are roughly sequential — finish most of one before the next will feel achievable — but tracks overlap, so dip ahead when you have the gear.
  • Every quest links to the guide with the full detail. Lost? Start at the progression roadmap for the big picture.
In-game tip

If the pack ships an actual FTB Quests book (it does — see the QoL guide), open it with its keybind/quest-book item; its rewards and order are authoritative for this pack. Use this page as a readable walkthrough alongside it.

Chapter I

Getting Grounded

GOAL — survive the first days with food, tools and a safety net.

  • Break ground. Gather wood, stone and your first iron. ↳ unlocks: a crafting base
  • Forge a Tinkers tool. Build a Part Builder + Tinker Station and assemble a pickaxe. [Tinkers] ↳ reward: an upgradeable, repairable tool
  • Cook a real meal. Set up a Cooking Pot + Stove and cook a stew. [Farmer's Delight] ↳ reward: long-lasting food buffs
  • Pack a bag. Craft a backpack. [Sophisticated] / [Traveler's] ↳ reward: carry capacity
  • Buy insurance. Confirm Tombstone graves work and craft a sleeping bag. [Tombstone] [Comforts] ↳ reward: death & night safety net

Chapter II

First Machines

GOAL — multiply your ore and store the surplus.

  • Build the Mekanism trio. Metallurgic Infuser, Enrichment Chamber, Energized Smelter for instant 2× ore. [Mekanism] [Assembly Line] ↳ reward: doubled ore + alloys/circuits
  • Light the first generator. A Heat Generator (lava) or a Create water wheel to power it. [Create] ↳ reward: scalable power source
  • Raise a Smeltery. Build a Tinkers Smeltery to melt ore into extra metal and cast parts. [Tinkers] ↳ reward: alloy tooling
  • Bulk storage. Stand up a wall of drawers with a controller. [Drawers] / [Functional] ↳ reward: tidy, countable stock

Chapter III

Power Up

GOAL — automate power and item flow across the base.

  • Brew ethylene. Feed a Gas-Burning Generator from a Pressurized Reaction Chamber. [Mekanism] ↳ reward: serious mid-game power
  • Go wireless. Distribute that power with Flux Networks. [Flux] ↳ reward: cable-free base power
  • Route the items. Wire machines with Pipez or an XNet network. [Pipez] [XNet] ↳ reward: hands-off processing
  • Searchable storage. Build a Tom's terminal, then start an AE2 network. [Tom's] [AE2] ↳ reward: one-search inventory

Chapter IV

The Arcane Arts

GOAL — pick up a magic track and forge real gear.

  • Bleed for power. Build a Blood Altar and bind your first orb. [Blood Magic] [Long Bleed] ↳ reward: the LP economy
  • Cast your first spell. Acquire a scroll and a spell book; pick a school. [Iron's Spells] ↳ reward: ranged magic
  • Enchant properly. Set up an Apotheosis enchanting table (Eterna/Quanta/Arcana) and socket a gem. [Apotheosis] ↳ reward: high-tier enchants + sockets
  • Stack modifiers. Cast Manyullyn/Hepatizon parts and load a weapon with modifiers. [Tinkers] ↳ reward: a boss-ready weapon

Chapter V

Into the Wild

GOAL — raid structures and beat your first real bosses.

  • Clear a dungeon. Loot a YUNG's or When Dungeons Arise structure, or descend Dungeon Crawl. [Exploration] ↳ reward: enchanted gear & XP
  • Open the Twilight. Build the portal and defeat the Naga. [Twilight Forest] ↳ reward: Naga Scale armour + progression
  • Slay a lair boss. Take down the Ferrous Wroughtnaut or a Mutant. [Mowzie's] [Mutants] ↳ reward: a unique boss weapon
  • Strike a deal. Trade with a Goblin Trader for something useful. [Goblin Traders] ↳ reward: convenience goods

Chapter VI

Industrial Might

GOAL — scale to the big multiblocks and autocrafting.

  • Go nuclear. Run a Fission Reactor into a Boiler + Industrial Turbine; bank it in an Induction Matrix. [Mekanism] ↳ reward: industrial-scale power
  • Automate crafting. Build AE2 Crafting CPUs and Pattern Providers. [AE2] ↳ reward: one-click crafting of anything
  • Don the MekaSuit. Assemble the MekaSuit + Meka-Tool and install modules. [Mekanism] ↳ reward: flight, auto-feed, vein mining
  • Unify the grid. If running IC2, bridge EU↔FE. [EU/FE Bridge] [IC2] ↳ reward: one shared power network

Chapter VII

To the Moon

GOAL — leave the planet (the whole point of the pack).

  • Build a rocket. Work up the Tier 1–4 rockets at the NASA Workbench. [Ad Astra] ↳ reward: spaceflight
  • Breathe off-world. Pack a space suit + Oxygen Distributor and seal a base. [Ad Astra] ↳ reward: survive the Moon
  • Plant a flag. Land on the Moon, then Mars, and mine Desh/Ostrum. [Ad Astra] ↳ reward: higher-tier rocket parts
  • Build a space station. Assemble an orbital base. [Ad Astra] [Building] ↳ reward: an off-world home

Chapter VIII

Apex Predator

GOAL — finish the boss ladders and reach the true endgame.

  • Finish the Twilight. Climb the boss ladder to the Snow Queen. [Twilight Forest] ↳ reward: top Twilight gear
  • Hunt the Cataclysm. Defeat the structure-bosses across the dimensions. [Cataclysm] ↳ reward: the pack's strongest weapons
  • Make antimatter. Run a Fusion Reactor + SPS and feed the Nucleosynthesizer. [Mekanism] ↳ reward: the endgame fabricator
  • Make it yours. Decorate, automate the last holdouts, and show off the base. [Building] [Chipped] ↳ reward: a finished world
Completion

Eight chapters, one rocket, and a wardrobe of boss gear later — you've "won" To the Moon 2.0. From here it's a sandbox: pick the track you love and push it to the limit. For the loose version of this path, see The Ascent.
