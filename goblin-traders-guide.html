The Goblin Market

Deep in the dark, a hunched little merchant is waiting to deal. Goblin Traders adds friendly underground goblins who buy your junk and sell oddly useful goods — a handy, mobile shop for the mid-game spelunker.

Underground traders Emerald economy MC 1.19.2 · Forge

00 — Orientation

Start Here

Two friendly goblin merchants spawn in the dark underground and offer trades you won't find at a villager. Run into one while caving and you've got a shop with no walking required.

This sits alongside the structures and dungeons in the Cartographer's Journal — goblins are something you bump into while doing everything else.

01 — The common one

The Goblin Trader

The standard Goblin Trader appears in dark underground spaces. He's non-hostile — approach and right-click to open his trade screen. He tends to hang around for a while, then wander or despawn, so deal when you find him.

  • Found in caves and dark cavities below the surface, often where light levels are low.
  • Offers a rotating set of trades — some for emeralds, some item-for-item.
  • Don't attack him: hitting a goblin makes it flee (and can turn the encounter sour).

02 — The rare one

The Vein Goblin

The Vein Goblin is the rarer, deeper-dwelling cousin, typically found further down in ore-rich depths. He carries a different, often more valuable trade list — worth seeking out once you're mining deep.

Treat spotting a Vein Goblin as a small jackpot: check his stock before he wanders off.

03 — The goods

The Trades

04 — Finding them

Spawning & Behavior

  • Where: dark underground areas; the Vein Goblin spawns deeper. Lighting up your base reduces goblins there — go find them in the unlit caves.
  • When: they appear over time as you explore; spawn rates are config-tunable.
  • Despawn: they don't stay forever — trade promptly. Some packs include a spawn/summon item approx if you want one on demand.
  • Disposition: friendly unless attacked. Leave them be and they'll keep trading.

05 — The value

Why Bother

Goblin Traders give you a villager-free shop right where you already are. While you're caving for ore (for Mekanism or Tinkers), you can offload junk for emeralds and pick up convenience items without building a trading hall. It's a small, pleasant economy loop that rewards exploring.

06 — Field notes

Tips

  • Keep a few emeralds and tradeable surplus on you while mining so you're ready to deal.
  • Always check both goblin types — the Vein Goblin's stock is the better one.
  • Never hit them — they flee and you lose the trade.
  • Trades vary by version and config; confirm specifics in JEI / in-game.
