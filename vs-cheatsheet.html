→ The spine

Stone → Steel, at a glance

The two gates that strand new players: (1) stone can't mine ore — cast a copper pick first; (2) a copper pick can't mine iron ore and a copper anvil can't work it — you need bronze for both. Full path: The Long Road.

⛏ Alloy ratios

Tin bronze · 88–92% Cu + 8–12% Sn
Bismuth bronze · 50–70 Cu + 20–30 Zn + 10–20 Bi
Black bronze · 70–90 Cu + 8–16 Au + 8–16 Ag
  • Strength: black > tin > bismuth > copper
  • ±0.1% outside range = melt fails

🛠 Tool / anvil tiers

  • 1 stone/flint — no ore
  • 2 copper — most ores, not iron
  • 3 bronze — adds iron ore
  • 4 iron — adds meteoric
  • 6 steel — everything
  • Anvil works its tier +1 above (bronze→iron, iron→steel)

🪨 Steel pipeline

  • Charcoal pit: seal <30s, ~18h
  • Bloomery: ≤120 ore → 1 bloom / 20 (max 6), ~10h
  • Hammer bloom → wrought iron
  • Cementation: 16 iron → 16 blister, ~6.67 days
  • Hammer blister → steel

🌱 Crop nutrients (N/P/K)

  • N: turnip, cabbage, spelt, rye
  • P: parsnip, onion, pumpkin
  • K: carrot, rice, flax, soybean
  • Rotate N→P→K · saltpeter/bonemeal/potash/compost

🧊 Cellar & preserving

  • Score: 100% − ≤40 skylight − ≤50 cooling; holds ~5°C
  • Sealed crock ×0.1 · vessel ×0.75 veg / ×0.5 grain
  • Salt barrel: 20-day cure → ~3.4 yr

🛡 Armor & combat

  • Flat reduction first, then %
  • Leather t1 60% · Gambeson t2 70% · Steel plate ~97%
  • Out-tiered: −0.2 flat / −15% per tier
  • Slots: body 50 · legs 30 · head 20 (hit%)
  • Predators wolf/hyena/bear = tier 2

🌅 Day-one checklist

  • Gather flint & sticks
  • Knap a knife (blade + stick)
  • Knap an axe → firewood
  • Firestarter (sticks+grass) → fire pit
  • A few torches (stick + dry grass)
  • Forage berries/roots + hunt a hare
  • Wall a shelter, fire inside
  • Survive the night → Day Two

Full walkthrough: The First Day
